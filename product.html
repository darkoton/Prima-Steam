Description:

ADCA DT40/2 series thermodynamic condensate traps are compact, reliable, simple and efficient in operation. They work perfectly in high-pressure systems, and can be used for drainage of steam lines outside the premises. The heat-insulating cover ensures uninterrupted operation and makes the trap suitable for use in such weather conditions as rain and wind, which can negatively affect the operation of the trap. These condensate traps have only one moving element - a stainless steel disc that ensures a clear, tight closure. Do not require adjustment in the entire range of workloads.

Main properties:

  • Intermittent condensate removal.
  • Automatic air removal.
  • Heat-insulating cover.
  • Works on superheated steam.
  • Disassembly in not required when replacing the disc and saddle.
  • Not susceptible to the effects of water hammer and vibrations.
  • Built-in filter that is easy to clean.

Working environments:

Satured and superheated steam.
